Delete Your Account

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

You can permanently delete your Voice account and the personal data associated with it at any time. This page explains how to request account deletion and what happens to your data afterwards.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

To delete your account directly from the Voice mobile app:

  1. Open the Voice app and log in to your account.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll to the account section near the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Read the confirmation message and tap Delete to confirm.

Your account will be deleted immediately and you will be logged out of the app.

Request Deletion Without the App

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion of your account and data by emailing us at support@voiceapp.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number, profile photo)
  • Your account credentials and login access
  • Your saved preferences and settings
  • Comments, proposals, survey responses, and other content you submitted

What May Be Retained

We may retain certain information for a limited period where required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, or enforce our agreements. Any retained information is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
